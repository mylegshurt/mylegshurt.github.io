Our Company

TSRCo is a technology development company founded in 2015 by then doctoral candidate, Christine Fanchiang. Orignially founded with the intent to focus on novel hardware for CubeSat missions, the company quickly branched into the areas of monitoring human performance through various wearable technologies. This prompted the development of supporting technologies surrounding the measurement, monitoring, and analysis of the human's operational state.

The company specializes in technologies supporting design, testing, and usage of advanced active wear technologies. In recent years the company has been working with NASA on a four-year research grant to develop algorithms that can detect useful information out of the complex and noisy signals associated with wearable performance systems.
