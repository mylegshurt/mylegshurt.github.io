Research Domains

Keeping people alive, healthy, and functional in extreme environments is no trivial pursuit. It requires a collective approach of appropritely designing for, measuring, and monitoring the health of the systems, operations, and the people involved. Our focus is in finding innovative ways to ensure that when we send humans into extreme environments, they are not only safe, but can perform at their best.

  • Advanced Active Wear

    Our company specializes in technologies supporting design, testing and usage of advanced active wear technologies. We build systems for life in the extremes.

  • Humans and Automation

    As missions become more reliant on automation, ensuring humans can work seamlessly with these new technologies is crucial. We provide research guidance on how to improve interfaces with automated systems.

  • Measuring Human Performance

    Measuring and interpreting the human's operational state can be very challenging but necessary for identifying changes to their well-being. We provide a customizable set of wearables and software to do just that.
